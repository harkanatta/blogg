--- title: "Ummig (about me)" author: "Valtýr" date: "09/12/2019" ---

Me, my wife and our three kids live in a little village called Skagaströnd in the north of Iceland. We came here for a job but have stayed longer than we expected (since the spring of 2015). Our kids have become proud villagers (skagstrendingar) and are absolutely opposed to moving anywhere else.

  • I work as a marine biologist at BioPol and I am also employed by the regional Nature center (in icelandic: Náttúrustofu Norðurlands vestra).
  • I have a BSc and MSc in biology from the University of Iceland. .
  • Assistant teacher in invertebrate zoology at the University of Iceland (spring 2014).
  • Summer jobs: Ice-cream truck driver, sement worker, after-school instructor….

Starfsmaður hjá BioPol á Skagaströnd og hjá Náttúrustofu Norðurlands vestra. Er með BSc og MSc í líffræði frá Háskóla Íslands.
